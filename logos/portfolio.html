Hello!

My name is Robert

I have been working as a freelance designer, specifically in designing interfaces and logos for use on the internet.

Skillset

I have multiple years of experience with creating multimedia with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, as well as creating interface mockups using the design prototyping platform InVision.

Examples of website mockups

Rimi is an Estonian grocery store with a mobile website that I found to be somewhat difficult to use and generally not very accessible on mobile devices.
The buttons were somewhat small and confusingly placed and there was quite a lot of unused screen space, whilst the rest of the design was somewhat tightly packed.

Thus I attempted to try and design a more accessible and aesthetically pleasing version of it using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InVision.
Whilst upholding to their general design aspects, I tried to use the space more efficiently and generally make navigating the website achievable without great effort.

Desktop mockup

Besides mobile web design, desktop websites are just as important. I designed the website and logo for a fictional restaurant using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InVision.
I attempted to give it a very fluid and free flowing interface, trying to keep things simple yet appealing.

Besides web design I also design logos and illustrations.
